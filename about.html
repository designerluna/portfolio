My Journey

Solving complex puzzles, digitally & physically.

I am a UX designer who pays scrupulous attention to detail and is always up for a challenge. Whether designing a workflow or mapping out a rock climbing route, I thrive on breaking down complex problems.

Outside of pixels and wireframes, I explore ways to test my limits—climbing vertical walls, running long routes, visiting new cities, and expressing visual forms through physical art.

Core Value

"Design is not just what it looks like. Design is how it functions, how accessible it is to everyone, and how easily a user can accomplish their goals when they are under cognitive load."
